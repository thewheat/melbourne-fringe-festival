Performance

The Substation - Studio

In the midst of a dense city Ann flies solo, addicted to observing her neighbours’ lives through her apartment window. When a tenant with a little girl moves in, Ann’s overjoyed. Until the man denies he has a daughter. She can no longer trust her own eyes. Dark fuzz blurs her vision. Are her eyes playing tricks or is a wrong being done?

Think Rear Window with Jimmy Stewart optically impaired. Ann is beset by dangers, not all of them outside her.  With atmospheric soundscape by Ekrem Mulayim (Food), lighting design by Christopher Page (The Dark Room) and a killer script, Dangerous Lenses is an eerie, gripping ride.

This urban Australian thriller imaginatively explores a predicament that collectively perplexes us.

Written by Brooke Robinson

Performed by Meredith Penman
