The Croft Institute

The Improv Conspiracy is Melbourne's leading source of "Chicago-style" improvised comedy.

After selling out their entire run in the 2013 Melbourne International Comedy Festival, The Improv Conspiracy is pleased to present A Night In Chicago, a brand new hour-long celebration of the Windy City's most impressive long-form improvised formats. Check the website for format descriptions and schedule! Every show is different!

This show features over 20 performers who have all come through The Improv Conspiracy's public workshop program, which offers formal improv training to Melbourne's arts community.
