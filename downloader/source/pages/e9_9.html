Lonergan and Ravens Funeral Home

Death? Café? Yes, that's right. Tea and mortality! Like it or not, one day you and everyone you know, are going to die. Maybe you have given this some thought, but have you discussed it? Death Café is a chance to share your hopes, concerns and questions over a beverage with a group of strangers.

From its origins in London, the global spread of Death Café reflects a growing trend in holistic approaches to death and dying. The fact our lives are finite need not be seen as a tragedy but essential to the value of life. So come, break bread with the undead...while you can.

Presented by Art of Dying
