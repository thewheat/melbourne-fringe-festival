The 101 Vagina Exhibition (and book) breaks down the taboo around vaginas and body-image shame generally. It is also about celebrating diversity and the sharing of story and wisdom.

The 101 Vagina Book comprises black and white photos of 101 womens' vaginas along with an accompanying message written by each woman from, or about, her vagina to the world.

The exhibition shows the book's content in large format; black and white photos of 101 vaginas along with a message written by each woman about her vagina.
