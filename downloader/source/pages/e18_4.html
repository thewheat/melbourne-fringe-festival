Visual Arts

Second Edition

Weston Frizzell is the collaborative identity of artists Otis Frizzell and Mike Weston.

Weston Frizzell's KUPU is a series of letter paintings featuring individual letters appropriated from iconic NZ artworks and brands, with an intention of creating art prints combining the letters into cliché Maori words dominant in the Kiwi-kitch genre of decorative art.

The first prints in the KUPU project were Aotearoa, Aroha, Home, and “World of Love” (Te Ao Aroha).

Produced by JoFF Rae
