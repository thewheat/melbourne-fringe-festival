As a child I dreamt of becoming a He-Man. As a teenager I dreamt of becoming a rock star. As a young adult I dreamt of becoming a working actor. It turns out destiny lay beyond my imagination...Simon Chugg is: Living the Dream.

Stand-up, music and multimedia from the country's favourite Zimbabwean-Australian bastard comedian. From the hilarious to the paranormal, the show centers on the experiences and struggles of an Afro-Australian artist in and around the Australian entertainment industry. Raising questions about socially accepted 'realities' of life, dreams and beliefs. And exploring topics including racism, addiction and fatherhood.

Created and Performed by Simon Chugg

Directed by Claire Harding
