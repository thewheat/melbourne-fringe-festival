Portland Hotel - The Locker Room

Hundreds of people have died throughout history. Thousands, even. Statistically speaking, at least a couple of these dead people must still be alive.

Comedian Tom McLean has enough eyes to know he's a seer; enough t-shirts to know he's a medium. He's never seen a ghost. He doesn't even believe in them. But they're real.

Join Tom for an hour of stand-up guaranteed to make you intangible with laughter. One hour of comedy; one litre of ectoplasm; one undeniable fact: Ghosts are cool.
