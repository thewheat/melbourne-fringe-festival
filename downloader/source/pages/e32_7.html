Cabaret

Fringe Hub - The Loft, Lithuanian Club

After the success of her debut show, Waitressing… and Other Things I Do Well, Gillian Cosgriff is doing less waitressing and a lot more of the other things. These may include: drinking, text messages, love, profanity, sex, dreams, humiliation, grammar, hangovers, and Band-Aids. Her hilarious and touching original songs will make you glad these things haven’t happened to you.

Written and Performed by Gillian Cosgriff
