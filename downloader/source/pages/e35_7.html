Australian Tapestry Workshop

Lilith defies God. Salome asks for John’s head. A young hipster’s heart is broken.  

Combining rich poetic language and intricate physical theatre, Yarn weaves together three timeless stories of loss and longing.
 
Devised and performed by Lily Fish (Inside A Mime's Compact, Alone, Isobel and Installation A) in residence at The Australian Tapestry Workshop, this is a unique site specific work not to be missed.
