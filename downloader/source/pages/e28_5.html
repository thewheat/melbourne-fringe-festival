Performance

La Mama

'Skitch is a natural storyteller. She’s confident, zany and really sweet, and even when she’s being meowww catty you still want to take her home and introduce her to your mum' - AussieTheatre

Indulge: Repair & nourish your inner-self.

Stimulate: Dream, believe, achieve.

Purifying Mask: Block out the rest of the world and ignore what’s really going on.

Parodying everyone from inner city workaholics to suburban dog owners, self-improvement junkies to newly-weds, Spoilt holds a mirror up to contemporary society to display some shattering results.

Trained at the school of Philippe Gaulier, Skitch is an expert mimic, clown and bouffon. 

'Skitch’s delivery is immaculately timed' - The Pun

Produced by deBASE productions and supported by Knox Community Arts Centre.

lamama.com.au/winter-2013/spoilt/

Written and Performed by Liz Skitch
