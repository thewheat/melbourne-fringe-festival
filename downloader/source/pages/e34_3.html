Circus

Gasworks - Theatre

Featuring hula hoops and bunny rabbits, Voila! is a show for the whole family. Children and parents alike will enjoy the fun as live animals magically appear and hoops are acrobatically tumbled through. Marvel at incredible circus skills and stunning feats of magic as the two art forms come together to create a fun, uplifting and passionate show.

Choreographed and Performed by Lee Cohen and Emma Shepherd
