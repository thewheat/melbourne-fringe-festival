Fringe Hub - The Loft, Lithuanian Club

***Due to popular demand, the performances on October 4th and 5th will now take place in the Main Theatre of the Lithuanian Club.***

Does it star some of Adelaide’s finest comedians? Is it a musical? Is it a parody of the Australian film Wolf Creek? Yes, yes, and pending legal approval, yes!


'Hilarious and genuinely entertaining...I laughed, I cried, I laughed again. It’s the feel good torture porn show everyone will love' - Greg McLean, director of Wolf Creek

'Bloody funny' - Rip It Up

'This is a good old-fashioned Fringe hoot...the crazy cast deliver deadpan lines that kill the crowd' -  Advertiser


"more laughs per minute than most television sitcoms." (Glam Adelaide)

Performed by Demi Lardner, James McCann, Kel Balnaves, Chris Knight, Hayman Kent and Angus Hodge
