The Theatre Husk

Drama With A Difference is an established and well respected Australian independent drama school. It has been the launching pad for many successful Australian actors.

The school is committed to using drama for recreational purposes and its classes for teenagers focus on building self-esteem in young people.

The Words of Others is a powerful series of short plays that focus on what it's like to be a teenager in today's Australia.

With input from the teenage student/actors, drama teacher/director/playwright Wayne Tunks has written a powerful series of short plays about growing up, bullying and self acceptance.
