Cabaret

Fringe Hub - Upstairs at Errol's

After a decade in showbiz 'The Queen of Kink' Gypsy Wood debuts her first solo show at the Melbourne Fringe! A regular at Fringe Festivals all over the world, and the co-producer of the smash hit show Comic Strip. Beauty Queens, Peanut Models and your favourite cabaret star John Howard as you have never seen him before.

'sexist and mean spirited – ah yes, the moral Left at play' - Andrew Bolt

'I was certainly not offended' - Former PM John Howard

'Doctors fear some spectators may never recover the full use of their scorched eyeballs' - The Times (UK)

Performed by Gypsy Wood
