***PLEASE NOTE both the 11am and 6pm performances on Saturday September 28th have been cancelled and will not go ahead***

 

After spending four years hosting hundreds of tours through the city of light, Jacqueline Mifsud owes it to humanity to give her fellow Melbournians a taste of Parisian history!

Come join Jacqueline as she recreates her historically accurate walking tour of Paris in the streets of Melbourne. 

Using her secret blend of pop culture references, vulgar charm and self-deprecating humour, Jacqueline gives you her personal guarantee that:

You’ll definitely be entertained

You’ll possibly learn something, and

You may demand a refund.

'I won't remember everything about my time in Europe, but I won't forget my tour with Jacqueline' - TripAdvisor reviewer Kallie, NY

*Please note this tour is not suitable for children or the politically correct.

Producer Jade Bullock

Performed and Devised by Jacqueline Mifsud
