Comedy

Fringe Hub - The Ballroom, Lithuanian Club

Critic’s choice, dual Barry nominee and gangly comedy mantis, Asher Treleaven presents The Experiment. An alternative Comedy Club for adventurous punters.

Guests will include co-host Geraldine Hickey; velvet dreamboat, Oliver Clarke; newcomer, Dayne Rathbone; body poet, Sabrina D'Angelo; Barry Winner, Randy; tropical delight, Xavier Michelaides; genius asshole, Rob Hunter and many more.

There will be a Caribbean house band, meta-comedy and a $15 prize for best small dog. BOOK NOW!
