Do you often ﬁnd yourself spending hours in front of a computer screen? Do you ever experience 'textiety' – that anxious feeling of waiting for a new text message? Do you get excited by the little red Facebook ﬂag that says ‘34 Notiﬁcations’? It’s okay if you do. I mean you could stop using technology any time. Couldn't you?

Fresh, fast-paced, futuristic fun for the whole family: join the attendees of a Technology Anonymous meeting on a buzzing ride through their lives as they delve into their technological addictions. Could you be addicted?

Squid Stamp, Melbourne's freshest theatre-in-education company delivers professional physical comedy for kids and adults alike. We educate through wildly entertaining performances, memorable characters and splendidly silly sketches!
