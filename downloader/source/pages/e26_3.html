The Sex Poetry Booth is back for sloppy seconds! Using the latest in psychosexual testing our sex-text expert Dosh Luckwell carefully crafts Sex Poems inspired by you. Winner of Best Live Art at Melbourne Fringe 2012, the Booth offers casual, on-the-spot sex poetry. From kissed to frisked, your confessions will be converted into take away poems you’ll want to read alone.

Dosh Luckwell is a professional pervert, published poet and pornography studies graduate who knows how to push the envelope through the slot. Getting your sweaty palms on your very own Sex Poem is easy. Simply have a quick flirt with Dosh at the Booth and he will bust one out for you! Last year was busy so get in quick!
