Fringe Hub - Upstairs at Errol's

Green Room Award Winners Backwards Anorak premier their new show Game of Thrones.

Wait. You can't call your show that? Okay. Um. Winter is Coming? Yes. Good. That. 

Dragons. Walls. Handsome beards. Incest. And the answer to the most asked question of the year - What song would Westeros select to represent their people at Eurovision? 

Created and Performed by Michelle Brasier and Vince Milesi
