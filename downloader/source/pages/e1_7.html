Court No 2, Supreme Court of Victoria

PLEASE NOTE THIS SHOW HAS NOW SOLD OUT

 

***NOTE: the performance commences at 7.30 NOT 8:00pm, as advertised in the program***

 A young man is charged with murder. 12 jurors must decide his fate.

BottledSnail Productions by arrangement with Origin Theatrical, exclusive representatives of the Dramatic Publishing Company of Woodstock Illinois, present Reginald Rose's inverted courtroom drama, with a cast made up entirely of barristers.

12 Angry Men pivots around the concept of 'reasonable doubt' and a group grappling with a difficult decision.  Set inside the towering stone walls of the Supreme Court of Victoria, this gripping, slow-burn production will challenge its audience.  What is guilt?  What is prejudice?  What does it really mean to do justice?


There are limited tickets available, so book early to avoid missing this unique and powerful production. 

 IMPORTANT:

- Gallery seating has restricted viewing and is accessed via a set of steep stairs with no lift access. Patrons experiencing mobility challenges can only be accommodated on the Ground floor.

- Entry to the Court is subject to security screening procedures. Guests are asked to arrive 30 minutes prior to the show to ensure security clearance ahead of a 7:30pm start time. Sharp objects, weapons, food and other consumables are not permitted inside the Court.

BottledSnail Productions is Melbourne's theatre company for the legal profession. We are proud to be raising money for the Tristan Jepson Memorial Foundation's work in combating depression and anxiety within the legal profession.

Director: Max Paterson

Assistant Director: Josh Glanc

Assistant Producers: Siobhan Toohey and David Barda
