Performance

Portland Hotel - The Locker Room

***PLEASE NOTE THAT THIS SHOW COMMENCES AT 10.15pm, NOT 10pm, AS LISTED IN THE PROGRAM***

 

At seventeen, best friends Tee and Alex had the world at their feet, but their plans for the ultimate road trip were totalled when Tee fell pregnant. Fifteen years later, Alex is back and they’re finally getting on that bus... And this time, the only route is the one they’re on. 

The truck stop cowboys might not be Brad Pitt, but this double denim duo are still on a full-throttle adventure. The stakes are high, the brakes are off and the fare’s not cheap (neither are the laughs).

Keep the pedal to the metal and your eyes on the horizon – there’s a whole lot of junk in this trunk. 

Created and Performed by Sarah Reuben and George Gayler
