Music

Long Play

The ultimate feast for the senses - emerging songstress and VCA graduate Freya Bennett combines her music with the photography of American artist Sophie Pelligrini in an extraordinary fusion of dulcet melodies, evocative moving image and film composition.

Combining the electricity of live band with a cinema screen of Pellegrini’s moving images, this season doubles up as the E.P launch for Bennet’s much anticipated debut album The Marvellous Reject Girl.

Freya Bennett is a Triple J unearthed artist, mentored by music veteran Steve Sedergreen and has been described as one of the most exciting emerging artists of the year. An evening not to be missed and to be relished by all.
