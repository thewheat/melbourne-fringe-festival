Show location info
Comedy

Gertrudes Brown Couch

Meet Lucy. She works as a clown. Meet Sarah, she's a comedienne who can’t tell jokes. Then there's Voula: a Greek girl with a hairy past. Tianne: bogan with a baby or two. Finally, Tessa and Tiffany: two torsos, three legs. Each has five minutes to convince their date they’re not as desperate/high/conjoined/related-by-marriage as they seem. Dating...not for everyone.

Lucy Gransbury (Dorothy Parker's Sweet Release of Death, sell-out season at Adelaide Fringe Festival 2013) and Sarah Jackson (Suitcases, Baggage & other Synonyms, sold-out at Melbourne International Comedy Festival 2013 and Melbourne Fringe Festival 2012) are joining forces to debut Tell Me About Yourself... There will be laughs. There will be wine. There may even be romance.

Written and Performed by Lucy Gransbury and Sarah Jackson

Directed by Nicholas Waxman
