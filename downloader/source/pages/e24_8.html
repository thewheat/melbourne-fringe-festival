Performance

Fringe Hub - The Loft, Lithuanian Club

One week only!

The critical smash-hit of the Melbourne International Comedy Festival returns!

Rama Nicholas’ one-woman Spaghetti Western Musical takes you on a journey into the heart of Mexico alongside an outlawed Cowgirl, a vengeful Spaniard and a mischief-making Horse!

Their mission? To take revenge on Death himself! 

Playing 15 characters, Rama will take you on a wild ride switching seamlessly between characters and songs to create this gutsy, hilarious, action-packed story!
