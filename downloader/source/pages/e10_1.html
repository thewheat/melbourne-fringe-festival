The Theatre Husk

Wanting, needing, desiring some 'one', some 'thing', some 'state'. Desire and Delirium looks at the human condition of desire in its various forms  - love, passion, status, fame, nirvana, fitness, beauty, material possessions - why do our desires so often drive us 'nuts'?

Presented by the adult students from Drama With A Difference- one of Australia's leading independent drama schools.

dramawithadifference.com.au

Co-devised by Drama With A Difference

Directed by Michele Williams
