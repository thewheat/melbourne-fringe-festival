Fringe Club - North Melbourne Town Hall

Following sell-out shows in New York, Montreal and Edinburgh, utterly dysfunctional siblings Otto and Astrid Rot return to Melbourne Fringe to host one night only of CIRCUS + DANCE + CIRCUS DISCOTHEQUE!

The circus and physical comedy stars of the Fringe will showcase their bendy skills to dance anthems from Fame and Flashdance through to Daft Punk and K-Pop.

With:

A4 Circus, Sabrina d'Angelo, Headfirst Acrobatics, Aerial Manx (Not Dead Yet) and the casts of Papillon and Voila!

Presented by Seesault
