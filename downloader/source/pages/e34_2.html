Viet Kieu, an Asian flavoured cabaret about an Australian Vietnamese woman precluded from being either truly Vietnamese or truly Australian.

A 12 year old girl returns home to Vietnam, her big Australian milk hips wowed the crowd. The people of Vietnam had never seen someone soooo huge and exotic. The stares. The gossip and the jealousy. She is an outcast. 

Viet Kieu a one woman show, performed by Diana Nguyen and directed by Felix Ching Ching Ho, promises a night of dark comedic tales through a repertiore of exotic recollections, saucy original songs and firecrackers.

*The term Viet Kieu refers to an Overseas Vietnamese.
