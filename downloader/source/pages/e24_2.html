What do you get when you mix Dr Who with Harry Potter and throw in a few Internet memes? A POP-MASHUP! The Doctor and his companion Hermione Granger join forces to defeat Tard the Grumpy Cat. Expect time travel, magic, and loads of laughs. Twenty percent cooler than your usual theatrical production...yay!

Written and directed by Katherine Phelps: writer for Nickelodeon Cartoons; Creator of the In Search of LOLitanium blog; lecturer in storytelling for computer game design; award-winning screenwriter.

'Somewhere between Dr Seuss and Shakespeare' - Splodgenoodles

'Playwright and comedian Katherine Phelps has to be commended on creating a time travel, magic and cat-infused musical comedy that will leave all geeks regarding it as the Shakespeare of their generation.'  5 Stars,  Avril Bylok-Collard - Beat magazine, 24 September 2013
