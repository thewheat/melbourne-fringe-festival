Bohemia Cabaret Club

Deep in the throbbing heart of South Melbourne a world of high eroticism and dark carnality pulses with life. Join caretaker Jasper Black and his extended 'family' as they share their wicked and depraved delights, offering up a sensual feast of cabaret, burlesque and fetish art.
