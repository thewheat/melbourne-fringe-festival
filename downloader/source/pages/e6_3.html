Blame it on Frederick is a unique murder mystery with live performance, experimental animation and video.

Time travelling detectives Marcy and Frobid are called in to uncover the mastermind responsible for the decline of western culture.

Carl Gustav Jung is kidnapped and asked to decode the symbolism of bad taste 1980’s music video clips.

Mayhem ensues.
