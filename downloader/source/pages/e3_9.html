Twas a foul night upon the moors when young Andy entered the abode of his friend, and confidant, Darcy. 

'Cheese?' he whispered, under his breath.

'Nacho Cheese' came the reply.

Having been been grown from a crystal by powerful monks, Andy met Darcy during a meteor shower in the spring of '97. Like the hit song of that year, Darcy believed he could fly. Physics would later prove otherwise.

After years of physiotherapy, Darcy moved in with Andy, who is slowly stealing his pencils.

Songs. Stories. Jokes. Magic. Cheese.
