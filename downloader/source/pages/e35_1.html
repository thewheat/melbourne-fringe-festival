A sketch show.

Wizard Sandwiches premiere a brand new show with fresh, fast-paced absurd sketches for the Melbourne Fringe. It's so new we cant even describe it. I'll try: no narrative, no theme, just silly sketches. How was that? Row AA* may get wet.

*There may not be a Row AA.

'This is absurd sketch, executed brilliantly. An absolute must' - YAWP Comedy Mag

'The Wizard Sandwiches do silliness very well...one of the best examples of sketch comedy I’ve seen' - Squirrel Comedy

'Utterly side-splitting sound effects, accents and facial expressions' -  Express Media
