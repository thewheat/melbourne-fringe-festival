Performance

Fringe Hub - Meeting Room - North Melbourne Town Hall

Ever have that moment when you’re telling a story and you realise hang on, that didn’t happen to me, that happened to my best friend or my dog or Meg Ryan in that rom-com… But you keep telling the story anyway?

A dark comedy about waiting: for a sign, for an escape, for a drink, for the baby to stop crying, for the snow to stop. 

From the writer of the 2010 Melbourne Fringe Award winning These are the Isolate.

'Warner’s play is full of rapid-fire word games - a compelling construction of a fractured interior nourished by Beckett...This is astonishing theatre. Four and a half stars ' Cameron Woodhead, The Age, review of These are the Isolate. 

toftheatre.wordpress.com

Written by Katy Warner

Directed by Prue Clark
