Comedy

The Tuxedo Cat - The Wild Pony

Stella Wilkenson is an 11-year-old performer, presenter, YouTube sensation, singer, dancer, actor, choreographer and pop-culture reference.

For too many years she has flown under the radar along with billions of other tween performers. And she's no spring chicken anymore so it's time for people to see how cutting edge she really is. She is edge. Edgy and talented. You'll all see. By the end of the show if Stella isn't immediately famous then there's obviously something completely wrong with the entire world. 

Grisabel Comedy cover their audiences 'in a fine film of hopes and dreams and sweet...affection' - Yawp. EDGE! will leave you feeling like you've had too much sugar. So a bit weird and shaky probably.
